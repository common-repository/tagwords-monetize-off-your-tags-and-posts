== Description ==
This plugin allows you to earn money with your tags. When your users click on your tags they are presented with advertiser products on your blog or get taken directly to the advertisers web site. You earn money for every sale or click depending on the tags and Advertisers configuration. We call advertising with your tags tagwords. Tagsoda can match tags with the right products avoiding spam on your site. Tagsoda can also suggest tags that match your content for optimal revenue potential.

Features
*Earn money by talking about Advertisers products
*Add tags to tagsoda
*Add posts to tagsoda
*Add tagwords to your posts
*List products related to tags from your posts.
*Sell your Posts
*Sell products from your blog
*Monetize on clicks and sales generated from your blog to advertisers web sites.


== Installation ==

Follow the steps below to install the plugin.

1. Upload the tagsoda directory to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to Settings/tagsoda to configure tagwords
4. Follow the rest of the instructions here <a href="http://tagwords.uintu.com/2010/05/17/tagwords-with-tagsoda-and-wordpress-documentation/">tagwords.uintu.com</a>
